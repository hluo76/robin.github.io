I am a third-year Ph.D. candidate in the Computer Science department at Northwestern University, advised by Professor Yan Chen and Han Liu in MAGICS lab.

My primary research interest lies in the intersection between Machine Learning, Health Care, and Financial Data.

Topics I am currently working on (Machine/Deep Learning):

  • Large Foundation Models:
    • Comprehensible Foundation Models, integrating modern Hopfield networks.
    • Responsible Foundation Model, encompassing jailbreaks, adversarial attacks, and risks associated with scientific foundation models. [1] [2]
    • Accessible Foundation Models, incorporating PEFT, quantization, and outlier removal for efficient training and enhanced quantization robustness. [1] [2]
    • Actionable Foundation Models, featuring Chain of Thought and Chain of Action methodologies. [1]
  • Applications of Large Foundation Models, including Genomic Foundation Models and Human Mobility Foundation Models.
  • Memory retrieval, memory-enhanced models, and memory editing techniques.

Topics I had worked on in the past (Before my PhD study):

  • Multimodality includes Video Question Answering and Visual Question Answering
  • Humman Robotic Interation
  • Reinforcment Learning
  • Natural Language Understanding
  • Question Answering

Besides the school and research work, I have found my interests in lots of things over time including photography, hiking and traveling.
